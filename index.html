About Me

I'm Ramon, a product designer from Barcelona with over 9 years of experience crafting digital experiences for agencies and brands. My passion lies in creating intuitive, user-centered designs that solve real problems while maintaining aesthetic excellence.


Currently working with Red Bull and teaching design at IronHack, I bring a unique perspective that combines industry expertise with educational insights. My background spans UI/UX design, brand identity systems, and digital product strategy.
